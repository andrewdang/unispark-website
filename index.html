Products

Emoticator EEG App

The Emoticator EEG application allows caregivers to understand, in pragmatic terms, mental commands and emotional state captured by a low-cost electroencephalogram headset.

mTable App

The mTable app is an interactive multiplication table application that allows users to touch and visualize math in a new way. Increasing math confidence is key for early development.

Sensory Spa VR App

A Sensory Integration tool that uses existing low-cost Virtual Reality technology to provide individuals with the means to have a "Sensory Room" wherever they are.

About

Founded in Eugene, Oregon, UniSpark is a Research & Development company that provides solutions for those with communication and mobility limitations. Through our subscription-based software and licensing, we provide the tools needed to bridge the gap between caregivers and those with autism and other special needs.

Feel free to reach out to us if you are curious about our products or services or to learn more about why we do what we do.

Advisors

Mor Nahum, PhD

Senior Scientist & Director of Research & Development

Dr. Mor Nahum's work focuses on web-based interventions and video games designed to improve social cognition in individuals with schizophrenia or autism. Dr. Nahum earned her PhD in Neural Computation at the Hebrew University of Jerusalem in Israel in 2009, and completed her post-doctoral fellowship at UC Berkeley in 2014. Her current work is supported by several awards from NIH, DARPA and the Simons Foundation.

Thomas Van Vleet, PhD

Senior Scientist & Director of Sponsored Programs

Dr. Thomas Van Vleet studies the mechanisms of attention and intrinsic alertness. He earned a dual doctorate in Neuroscience and Clinical Psychology at Northern Illinois University, with additional training in Neuropsychology at the University of Chicago. Dr. Van Vleet completed a research fellowship at the University of California, Berkeley and is a licensed Neuropsychologist.
